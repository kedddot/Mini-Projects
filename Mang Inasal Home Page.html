Mang Inasal is a popular Filipino fast-food chain renowned for its grilled chicken, particularly its signature "Inasal" chicken, marinated in a blend of spices and char-grilled to perfection. Founded in Iloilo City, Mang Inasal offers a variety of other traditional Filipino dishes, including pork BBQ, halo-halo, and palabok, in a casual, family-friendly setting. The restaurant is celebrated for its affordable prices and fast service, making it a beloved choice for both locals and visitors craving authentic Filipino flavors.
