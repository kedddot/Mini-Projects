About Us

Mang Inasal is a Filipino fast-food chain that specializes in grilled chicken served in a “pinakbet” or a spicy Filipino vegetable stew. Mang Inasal was founded in 1996 by Alvin Yu and George T. Tiangco, and has since expanded to over 1,000 locations worldwide. The company is known for its signature “Inasal” style of cooking
